Glitch Art

The images I chose to distort for this project are of events that most people are expected to experience if they live their lives the "right" way. I think in
American culture, we put a lot of pressure on people to get an education, get married, and have children. Society tends to judge you if you don't do these
things and in this exact order. Distorting these images represents that not everybody decides to live according to the same plan and that there is nothing
wrong with that. Everybody's experience is unique.
