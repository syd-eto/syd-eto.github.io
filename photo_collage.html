Just a Counrty Apple in the Big City


10 February, ​2023

This collage is bursting with excitement as rays of color burst out from apples, which have become public spectacle. It's a fun way to express
how I felt moving from a farm in San Luis Obispo to a big city like San Jose. I can feel myself changing, and while it is exciting I'm
not always sure is for the better.
