Family History

I created these images in the spirit of Thanksgiving. It's one of the few times my grandpa will talk about the struggle my family went through when they first emigrated to
America. The reason being that my great grandfather expressed a wish for our family to let go of the past and look toward the future despite all of the pain he had been
through. I chose the Midjourney Ai generator to express my family history because it was a good metaphor for how our perceptions of events are colored by our
upbringings. My family fed me information about our past and that in turn created pain and resentemnt in me for events that I never experienced myself. And I was taught
forgive and look forward by a man I never met. Incidentally, I never told the Ai generator to have the subjects looking out toward the horizon. It did that on it's own.


Three Brothers


20 November, ​2022

The first image is of my great great grandfather and his two brothers getting off of a boat in San Fransico. Each brother had their own aspirations and went
off on their own. One of them went to the south, the other to washington settling down is Seattle, and my great great grandfather purchased land in San Luis
Obispo to start farming.


Farming Hope


20 November, ​2022
The second image is of my great great grandfather showing my great grandfather and my great uncle the ropes of working on our family farm. They were
taught to value hardwork and generosity.




Taken Away


20 November, ​2022
Suspicious of the my great grandafther for helping many Japanese immagrants settle in America the U.S. government threw him in Federal prison for
esionage. The last image depicts my great aunts and uncle being sent to concentration camps during World War II where my grandpa was born.
