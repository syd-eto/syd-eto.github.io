Baiji

Added to our collection in 2017 the Baiji, sometimes called the Chinese river dolphin, is a pale blue-gray dolphin that grew up to 8 feet long.
We captured it by damaging it's environment,the Yangtze River, with dam construction, river pollution, overfishing, and boat traffic.
