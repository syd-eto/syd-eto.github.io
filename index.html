About Me

Howdy!


Sydney Eto hails from San Luis Obispo, California. As a child, she took a lot of inspiration from watching old anime and 80s movies.
You can see her passion for bright color palettes in her artwork. Sydney is currently a San Jose State University student in the DMA department.
Growing from her background in graphic design, she enjoys designing layouts and prototyping applications. She hopes to create new artworks using 3d modeling and animation techniques.
