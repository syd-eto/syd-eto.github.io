African Elephant

Deep in the dense, humid forests of West and Central Africa, you used to be able to find the elusive forest elephant. Now you can find them here! There natural habitat was lost to poaching, agriculture, and human conflict in general. In 2040 they practically walked into our online Menagerie and we're happy to have them.
